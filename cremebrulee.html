Creme Brulee

Creme brulee is a classic French dessert that consists of a smooth and creamy custard topped with a thin layer of caramelized sugar. The sugar is torched or broiled to create a crunchy and golden crust that contrasts with the soft and rich custard. Creme brulee is a decadent and elegant dessert that can be flavored with vanilla, chocolate, coffee, or fruit.
