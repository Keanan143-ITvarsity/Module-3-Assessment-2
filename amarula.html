Amarula

Amarula is a creamy liqueur made from the fruit of the marula tree, native to South Africa. It has a rich and smooth taste, with hints of caramel, vanilla, and chocolate. Amarula can be enjoyed on its own, over ice, or mixed with coffee or hot chocolate. It is also a popular ingredient in desserts, such as cheesecake, tiramisu, and ice cream.
