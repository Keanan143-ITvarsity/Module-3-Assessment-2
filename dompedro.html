Dom Pedro

Dom Pedro is a classic South African drink that combines ice cream, whipped cream, and a shot of whiskey, brandy, or liqueur. It is served in a tall glass with a straw and a sprinkle of chocolate shavings. Dom Pedro is a perfect way to end a meal, or to cool off on a hot day. You can choose your favorite flavor of ice cream and liquor, or try something new, such as mint, coffee, or Amarula.
