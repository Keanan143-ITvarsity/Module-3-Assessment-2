Passion fruit and lemonade

Passion fruit and lemonade is a refreshing and fruity drink that is easy to make and enjoy. All you need is some fresh or canned passion fruit pulp, some lemonade, and some ice. You can also add some mint leaves, sugar, or honey for extra flavor. Simply mix the passion fruit and lemonade in a pitcher, pour over ice, and garnish with mint. This drink is ideal for a summer picnic, a brunch, or a party.
